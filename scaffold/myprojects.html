Listed below are a couple of the projects I worked on while attending Missouri State University.




VIRTUAL CASINO

The Virtual Casino is a gaming app I created for Android devices. The idea behind the project was to emulate multiple traditional casino games and allow the player to bet and play as they would in real life. The Virtual Casino app has three game modes including Slots, Roulette, and Blackjack.



FALL DETECTION SYSTEM

The Fall Detection System is software designed to work in the homes of the elderly or crippled individuals. The system works by having a camera monitor a room and watch to see if any people are in the room and what "state of being" they are in. The system uses a database of templates that are compared to the live picture to determine if a person in frame is standing, sitting, falling, or lying down. Using an algorithm that takes into account various measurements such as distance, speed, lighting, and position. We can determine if a person has fallen down and get them the help they need.
